Youth Speak Forum 2021

Youth Speak Forum is an event organized by AIESEC that takes place in multiple locations around the world, bringing top young leaders together with business leaders, thought leaders and experts for a conversation around youth contribution to the globe. The aim is to generate actionable ideas that will impact the world. We make this happen by developing our forum programme through the unique interactive framework of Empower, Engage, Commit.

Youth’s role is essential as for the nation and global. With the current pandemic and economic recession, youth are at a disadvantage as the opportunity and situation has restricted them to unleash their leadership potential and create sustainable impacts for the community. Moreover, this situation also affecting Youth mental health which can lead to a serious matter. Therefore, it is important to cultivate the youths to unleash themselves by adapting to the situation and utilising their advantages in this digitalized era. Through Youth Speak Forum 2021, youths are able to develop themselves while enhance their thinking skills and self-confidence to be an outstanding leader with a healthy mental status in the workplace.

What are you waiting for? Check out the Youth Speak Forum 2021 booklet in @aiesecinupm bio and find the registration link there! Stay tuned for the upcoming interesting topics in Youth Speak Forum 2021!
